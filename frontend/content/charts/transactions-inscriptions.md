Shows the daily number of transactions that reveal at least one inscription.

<!--more-->

A transaction is counted as an inscription transaction when at least one of its inputs reveals an [ordinals inscription](https://docs.ordinals.com/inscriptions.html) via a Taproot script-path spend.
